Highers and Advanced Highers

During my high school years at Stonelaw, I developed a deep range of core skills within English, Mathematics, and Science. Much of the work and learning I was given here rooted much of my passions today, some newly founded and others excited. I remember fondly some of my memories of high school, they ultimately played a key role in building my work ethic which gave me much strength in working towards harder tasks during my time in university.

My six years at Stonelaw had me complete not only basic National Qualifications but Scottish Higher Qualifications also, completing five highers to a passing standard isn't an easy feat and one in which I pride myself still to this day, some results I had hoped would've turned out better but this only instilled a sense of 'do or die' at university. Albeit still impressive to pass all my highers first try.

Moving forward, my penultimate year at Stonelaw seen me take two advanced highers and a resit of one higher I wished for a better result in. Advanced computer science, physics, and higher chemistry. I failed physics unfortunately, the teaching staff hadn't taught the newest advanced course and the last time they had was four to five years prior. However, I passed compter science easily, and improved my chemsitry grade.
