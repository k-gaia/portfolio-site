Kevin the Retail Assistant

Over the summer of 2019. I held a role in Argyle Street Primark, it was a highly enjoyable, fast-paced role. In which I found many challenges I hadn't faced yet in my early career. I had only been a temporary ticket vendor through an agency at the time, and having to handle many more customers was completely new. After my training I came to love it, I enjoyed my time at Primark as my colleagues were lovely, and the customers too. I always made sure anyone I helped, walked out of my store with whatever they needed.
